Hailey's healthy glowing skin raises expectations for Rhode. The term glazed donut, used for bright and smooth skin appearance, inspires Rhode. Why did Hailey name her skincare Rhode? After considering a line inspired by her marital surname, the model has reportedly turned to her middle name Rhode (her full name is Hailey Rhode Baldwin Bieber) for her latest business venture. It's a move that her husband made when creating his fashion label Drew House, taking inspiration from his middle name Drew.
