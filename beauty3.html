EVERYTHING ABOUT RIHANNA'S MAKEUP COLLECTION FENTY BEAUTY!

The packaging designs are designed in a hexagonal shape, inspired by honeycomb. First of all, Fenty Beauty's packaging and products are produced with great care and high quality. Thanks to the hexagonal magnetic design, all products can be interlocked with each other. This means convenience and elegance both on the make-up table and in the make-up bag.

The series includes foundation in 40 different shades, transparent powder, eye shadow, highlighter, stick products that can be used for contouring, makeup brushes, lip glosses, bases, oil-absorbing wipes and a makeup sponge.

Fenty Beauty actually belongs to the LVMH group, which bought Rihanna's Fenty name rights. It is said that the group, which includes dozens of world-famous luxury brands such as Louis Vuitton, Bulgari, Fendi, Givenchy and Dior, paid Rihanna $10 million for this collaboration.
