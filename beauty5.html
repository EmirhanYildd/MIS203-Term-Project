BEAUTY

Kylie Jenner, who quickly entered the beauty world with her double lipstick kits, established a real make-up empire with the subsequent product families, and became the world's youngest billionaire, continues with Kylie Skin after Kylie Cosmetics!

Kylie Jenner said, I created Kylie Cosmetics to give my fans access to the makeup products I love and use every day to create my own looks. Whether I have my make-up done with my attractive team for shoots or I do my own make-up at home, I love how make-up makes you feel confident and beautiful, and I want to share this feeling with you.

In 2014, Kylie Jenner and her mother Kris Jenner founded the company. The company's first product is the liquid lipstick and lip liner Kylie Lip Kits. Kylie Jenner described her decision to use her former insecurities about lip size as inspiration for her brand: "This is the most original thing I've ever done in my career."

Great makeup starts with good skin, and Kylie Jenner dreamt up Kylie Skin soon after launching Kylie Cosmetics.
