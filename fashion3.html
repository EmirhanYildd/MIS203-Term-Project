Rihanna Made Her Mark on the Formula 1 Race with Her Style!

Stars such as Brad Pitt, Kylie Minogue, Paris Hilton and J Balvin were among the A-list participants at the Formula 1 Las Vegas Grand Prix. Rihanna and A$AP Rocky also appeared; While Rocky unveiled a special Puma x F1 collaboration, Rihanna showed off two stunning looks over the weekend, both of which put her stamp on her racing style.

Rihanna's first F1 look featured a long brown leather jacket with utility panels from Prada's spring 2024 collection. To balance this, she piled her style with diamond accessories that would make her more evening-ready. The next day, the superstar continued her focus on leather with a second race-inspired look. After all, F1 drivers wear leather too. She wore an oversized biker jacket from Balenciaga's spring 2024 collection and paired it with Demna's signature Pantaboots and futuristic Ambush sunglasses.
