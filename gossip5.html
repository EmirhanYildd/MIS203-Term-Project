KYLIE JENNER'S NEW FASHION BRAND!

Kylie Jenner announced that she will establish a new fashion brand. Little is known about the brand yet. The name of the brand is "khy". She announced the brand to everyone by sharing it on Instagram.

There is not much information about the brand yet. The brand has a website, but it only has a photo of Kylie Jenner from behind and the option to register. There are no details on the brand's Instagram account. The account has the same photo and the text "coming soon. sign up at khy.com" as the description.

Users can register on the site for early access to the collection, but the release date is uncertain. It didn't surprise anyone that Kylie Jenner would launch a clothing brand. Many of her sisters already own very successful fashion businesses. At the same time, Jenner already has a successful cosmetics brand. She is very successful in running a business.
